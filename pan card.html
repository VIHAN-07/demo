PAN Card Application Guide

Step 1: Fill PAN Card Application Form

Download Form 49A from the NSDL or UTITSL website and fill it accurately.

Step 2: Attach Required Documents

Attach documents like proof of identity, address, and date of birth.

Step 3: Submit Application

Submit the application form along with the required documents to the nearest PAN center.

Step 4: Pay Application Fee

Pay the prescribed fee online or through demand draft along with the application.

Step 5: Track Application Status

Track your application status online using the acknowledgment number provided.
