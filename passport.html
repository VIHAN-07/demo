Indian Passport Application Guide

Step 1: Register Online

Create an account on the Passport Seva website and fill in the application form.

Step 2: Gather Documents

Collect required documents such as proof of identity, address, and date of birth.

Step 3: Schedule Appointment

Schedule an appointment at the nearest Passport Seva Kendra or Passport Office.

Step 4: Visit Passport Office

Visit the Passport Office on the scheduled date with all required documents.

Step 5: Biometric Data Capture

Your photograph, fingerprints, and signature will be captured at the Passport Seva Kendra.

Step 6: Fee Payment

Pay the applicable fee for passport processing through online or offline mode.

Step 7: Passport Issuance

Once your application is processed, your passport will be issued and dispatched to your address.
