Voter ID Application Guide

Step 1: Fill Voter ID Application Form

Obtain and fill the Form 6 provided by the Election Commission of India.

Step 2: Attach Required Documents

Attach documents such as proof of age, address, and identity along with passport-sized photographs.

Step 3: Submit Application

Submit the completed application form along with the necessary documents to the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO) or Booth Level Officer (BLO).

Step 4: Verification

After submission, your application will be verified by the designated authority.

Step 5: Receive Voter ID

If your application is approved, you will receive your Voter ID card by mail or you can collect it from the designated location.
